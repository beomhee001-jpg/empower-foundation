Partner Spotlight

Boys & Girls Club of Stanton

Our favorite kind of partnership — the kind we keep coming back to.

Some of our favorite chapters have been written at the Boys & Girls Club of Stanton. We've partnered with the Club multiple times — and we keep coming back, because the energy in that building is exactly why Empower Foundation exists.

Across our visits, our volunteers have run hands-on STEM workshops, hosted reading and homework-help sessions, set up game and activity afternoons, and pitched in on supply drives and seasonal celebrations. Every visit is planned with the Club's staff so our time meets a real need — and every visit ends with our team already talking about the next one.

Stanton's kids show up curious, competitive, and full of questions. Our job is simple: keep showing up for them.

"Every visit reminds us why we started — kids light up when someone keeps showing up for them."

What volunteers do at the Club

  • STEM workshops — hands-on builds, experiments, and science demos
  • Reading buddies — one-on-one reading and homework help
  • Games & activities — art stations, group games, and friendly tournaments
  • Drives & events — school supplies, books, and seasonal celebrations
