Every event has one goal: give Orange County kids a hands-on experience they'll remember. Here's what we've been up to.

1 Event 01

STEM Volunteering at the Boys & Girls Club of Stanton

Stanton, CA Spring 2026

We spent the day with the kids at the Boys & Girls Club of Stanton showing them that STEM isn't a textbook — it's something you get your hands on. Our volunteers ran stations where kids made their own slime, engineered LEGO builds, learned chess strategy, and tackled hands-on building challenges. Every activity snuck real science, engineering, and problem-solving into an afternoon that just felt like fun.

2 Event 02

Empower Book Drive

Orange County, CA Community drive

Our team gathered in a local neighborhood and went door to door collecting book donations from the community. Every book we gathered was donated to local charities, nonprofits, and libraries — putting stories back into circulation and into the hands of kids and families across Orange County. A simple idea, a full trunk of books, and a lot of neighbors who wanted to help.
