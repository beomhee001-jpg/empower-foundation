Before You Start

Volunteering Starts With Membership

To volunteer with Empower Foundation, you first need to become a member. It's free, takes about two minutes, and it's how we keep every kid we serve safe and supported.

1 · Fill Out the Form

Complete our short membership form — your name, contact info, and a couple of quick questions. About two minutes.

2 · Get Confirmed

We'll review your form and welcome you as an official Empower Foundation member.

3 · Start Volunteering

Once you're a member, you can sign up for STEM workshops, reading sessions, and club visits across Orange County.

Become a Member

Membership is required to volunteer with Empower Foundation. Fill out the membership form to join — it only takes a couple of minutes.
